Alerts

Add dismiss functionality to all alert messages with this plugin. When using a .close button, it must be the first child of the .alert-dismissible and no text content may come before it in the markup.Just add data-dismiss="alert" to your close button to automatically give an alert close functionality. Closing an alert removes it from the DOM. To have your alerts use animation when closing, make sure they have the .fade and .in classes already applied to them.
